# General definition: what is 
[from Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_quality)

<Horizontal>
    <div><span style={{ fontWeight: 'bold'}}>Business and Product Owners</span>:<br/>Functional quality reflects how well it complies with or conforms to a given design, based on requirements or specifications</div>    
    
    <div><span style={{ fontWeight: 'bold'}}>Developers and Ops</span>:<br/>Structural quality refers to how it meets non-functional requirements that support the delivery of the functional requirements, such as robustness or maintainability​</div>

# Metrics: Management process
<ul>
    <li>lead time: from TODO to DONE</li>
    <Steps>
    <li>number of bugs ➡️ defect density ➡️ customer satisfaction</li>
    <li>committment/actual completion ratio ➡️ team reliability</li>
    <li>sprint burndown velocity ➡️ team internal organization</li>
    <li>team velocity ➡️ team productivity</li>
    </Steps>
</ul>

*Tools*: JIRA reports

<ul>
